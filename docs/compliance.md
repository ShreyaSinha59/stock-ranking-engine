# Compliance, Licensing & Disclaimers (Phase 1.1.8)

This document defines compliance and licensing rules for data usage and user-facing outputs.

---

## Disclaimer

This system is for educational and research purposes only and does not constitute investment advice.
No guarantees are made regarding performance or returns.
Users are responsible for their own decisions.

---

## Data Source Usage

- SEC EDGAR filings may be stored and processed.
- Macroeconomic data may be stored with attribution.
- News content must be stored as metadata only (headline, timestamp, source, URL) and not as full articles.

---

## Attribution

All data sources must be attributed in the dashboard and documentation.

---

## Privacy

The system must not store user PII or brokerage account data.
The public API and dashboard are read-only.

---

## Output Framing

Outputs are rankings based on signals and constraints, not buy/sell instructions.
